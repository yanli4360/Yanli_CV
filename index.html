Biography

我是
I am currently a Professor at School of Cyberspace Science and Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), P. R. China. I received the B.S. degree in computer science and technology and the B.S. in finance from Shandong University in 2009, and the Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua University in 2014. I was a visiting scholar at University of Massachusetts Amherst. More details about full publications can be found in Google Scholar and DBLP.

Opening positions. I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D./master students to join my group, and accept senior undergraduate students to the research group for internship. Please email me ( dianl@bit.edu.cn) your CV if you are interested.

Research Interests

  • Data Security: Research on data security sharing technology, machine learning data privacy protection mechanism, applied to smart medical, Internet finance and other fields.
  • Artificial Intelligence Security: Research on vulnerability analysis and security enhancement technology for AI technologies such as deep learning, federated learning, and applications (e.g., face recognition systems, voice recognition systems).
  • Network Encrypted Traffic Analysis: Research on using deep learning techniques to analyze the regularity and characterization information in network traffic (especially encrypted traffic) for applications in network anomaly detection, malicious website identification, cyberspace resource detection, and encrypted video service optimization.
  • Blockchain Security: Research on abnormal transaction identification and traceability technology of blockchain digital currencies to guarantee the supervision and security protection of the new decentralized network computing model.
